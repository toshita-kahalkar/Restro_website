About Us

We speak the Good Food language

The Cafe & Roof Top Restaurant restaurant is located at Bhandara near J. M. patel college This fine-dining multi-cuisine restaurant has elegant yet simple indoor ambience with a beautiful outside view and they also have terrace dining area too.

The Cafe has been a prime location to celebrate Kids Birthday Party, Birthday Party, Anniversary and many other events. Restaurant provides numerous facilities like Power Backup, Lift, Air Condition, In House Catering and many more which ensures that your event is successful.
